# Customer Success Case – aniTime

## Overview
aniTime is a new toy and collectibles store based in Vancouver.

I was brought in at the very beginning to build the business foundation from scratch — including the ecommerce website, customer lifecycle system, and marketing setup.

This was not an optimisation project. It was a zero-to-one build.

---

## Problem

- No website or ecommerce infrastructure  
- No CRM or customer lifecycle system  
- No structured onboarding or retention strategy  
- No internal capability to manage tools or marketing  

---

## What I Built

### 1. Full Ecommerce Website (Shopify)

- Built the entire website from scratch using Shopify (Empire theme)  
- Structured collections, product pages and navigation for scalability  
- Implemented preorder functionality to support inventory model  
- Added SEO meta tags and collection structures to improve discoverability  
- Integrated apps and workflows to support operations  

---

### 2. Customer Lifecycle & CRM System

- Set up Klaviyo from scratch  
- Built lifecycle flows: welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase  
- Created segmentation logic (new vs returning customers)  
- Established a repeatable campaign structure  

---

### 3. Acquisition & Growth Setup

- Launched Meta and Google Ads campaigns  
- Structured campaigns based on product categories  
- Connected tracking across platforms for visibility  

---

### 4. Internal Enablement

- Built the operational foundation for the team  
- Trained staff to manage Shopify, Klaviyo and daily operations  
- Set up Instagram foundation and content direction  
- Ensured the business can run independently after handover  

---

## Customer Success Impact

### Onboarding → Adoption
Customers now enter a structured journey instead of a one-time transaction.

- Website designed to guide product discovery  
- Lifecycle flows ensure customers stay engaged after purchase  

---

### Retention
The business now has a system to drive repeat purchases.

- Post-purchase engagement flows  
- Segmentation enables more relevant communication  

---

### Visibility
The team can now make decisions based on data.

- Clear tracking of customer behaviour  
- Better understanding of what drives conversion  

---

## Outcome

- Built a fully functional ecommerce and customer system from zero  
- Enabled the business to operate independently  
- Created a scalable foundation for growth and retention
